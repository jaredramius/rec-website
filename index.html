Introduction to Recollective

  • Intuitive features

    Simple enough for first-time online researchers to quickly pick up and use, with a comprehensive suite of features to explore any topic in depth, Recollective is the perfect platform for both agency and client-side researchers.

    Features

  • Do It Yourself

    Ready to do-it-yourself? Recollective’s DIY research solution comes with all the training and support you need to launch a successful online project. Our fabulous Services team can even help you with recruiting, programming and more.

    Services

  • Online Research

    Unlock research capabilities beyond old bulletin board technology. Recollective combines the latest in activity-based research, online journals for ethnography and live-chat forums in one seamless participant and admin experience.

    Applications

  • Mobile Ready

    Effortless mobile research requires a platform designed with mobile in mind. Recollective’s industry-leading responsive design automatically works across all platform and device types so you can focus on the research itself.
